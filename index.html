Chicken Katsu

Ingredients

Chicken Breasts 500 grams
Flour 1 cup
Eggs - beaten 2 large
Panko crumbs 1 cup
Oil 50 mls

Method

Coat the chicken breasts in flour, shaking off any excess. Dip them into the egg, and then press into the panko crumbs until well coated on both sides. Heat 1/4 inch of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Place chicken in the hot oil, and cook 3 or 4 minutes per side, or until golden brown.
